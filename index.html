Welcome to Kaalmo School

Kaalmo School is a center of learning and leadership based in Mogadishu, Somalia. Our vision is to provide every child with the opportunity to succeed through quality education and strong values. Since our founding in 2010, we have grown into one of the most respected private schools in the region.

We believe education is the foundation of a strong and successful community. At Kaalmo School, students learn to think critically, communicate clearly, and solve real-world problems. Our goal is to prepare students not just for exams, but for life.

Our Facilities

  • Modern Classrooms
  • Science and Computer Labs
  • Library and Study Areas
  • Playgrounds and Sports Fields
  • Digital Learning Tools

Achievements

Our students consistently perform well in national exams and academic competitions. Many of our graduates have gone on to attend top universities locally and internationally.
